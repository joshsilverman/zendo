Manifesto

Background: StudyEgg is a place to create, share and sell adaptive flashcards and adaptive learning games for college courses and popular textbooks. The flashcards and games are delivered on a student's smartphone according to the student's individual needs.

Core belief:

    I believe that most college students like learning. However college is full of distractions and studying is often pushed off. As midterms and finals approach, students end up frantically cramming as much info into their heads as possible. It disappears almost as quickly as it is learned.

My people:

  • My people are frustrated by this ineffective learning process. Studying feels stressful and the result of studying does not feel like learning.

Invention:

  • My invention is personalized flashcards/games that accompany textbooks and courses. Students can learn through a steady stream of activities customized to their needs and strengths, instead of paging through textbooks and slidedecks.

Commitment to our people:

  • No Gatekeepers - Users who wish to produce their own learning resources for use with our adaptive engine will have full access to our tools.
  • Public Domain - We will facilitate public domain initiatives that seek to disseminate high quality learning materials to schools and communities.
