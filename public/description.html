Description and Direction

Product Summary: StudyEgg delivers a stream of flashcards and short learning exercises to students for their courses and textbooks. Students receive short activities on their smartphones according to their individual needs.

Barriers to learning:

    We believe that most students like learning. However student lives are full of distractions and studying is often pushed off to the last minute. As assessments approach, students end up frantically cramming as much info into their heads as possible. It often disappears as quickly as it is learned.

Frustration with the status quo:

  • Many students are frustrated by this ineffective learning process. Studying often feels stressful and the result of studying does not feel like learning.

Our goal:

  • We intend to help students learn more in college and high school with less stress surrounding the study process. By making studying easier and delivering personalized content specific to individual needs, students will prepare more easily for quizes and exams and learn much more.

Our approach:

  • Our tool is personalized flashcards/games that accompany textbooks and courses. Students can learn through a steady stream of activities customized to their needs and strengths, instead of paging through textbooks and slidedecks.

Commitment to our people:

  • No Gatekeepers - Students who wish to produce their own learning resources for use with our adaptive engine will have full access to our tools.
  • Public Domain - We will facilitate public domain initiatives that seek to disseminate high quality learning materials to schools and communities.
